
Meet Hardware Finder

We’ve all been there — mid-project, excited to create… and that one tool disappears. Frustration builds, time slips away, and you end up buying a replacement — only to find the original later.

Enter Hardware Finder — built by makers, for makers. Instantly store, organize, and find every tool, bolt, and part you own. No more wasted time, no more duplicates — just freedom to create.

Your data stays local and private, with easy import/export as hardware.db or hardware.csv. Safe, portable, and always under your control.

With Hardware Finder, your workspace is stress-free, organized, and ready for flow. When everything has its place, creativity has no limits — and every project feels effortless.
